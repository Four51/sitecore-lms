FAQ

  • How can I purchase on behalf of others on my team?
    • Each learner will need a Sitecore Learning account in order to successfully checkout.  Add 1 or more  of each course you would like to purchase. At checkout, provide the email addresses of each learner. Once your order is completed, each learner will find the training on their Sitecore Learning accounts.
  • Does the person for whom I am buying the product need to already have an account in the system?
    • Yes. If they do not already have an account, please have them create one here: https://www.sitecore.com/account/signup
  • What should I do if the user I am purchasing on behalf of is not found in the system durring checkout?
    • If the learner you are trying to purchase training on behalf of is not found in the system, please have them create a Sitecore Learning account here: https://www.sitecore.com/account/signup. After that, please refresh your cart and proceed to checkout.
  • Can I purchase Sitecore training only on behalf of others and not for myself?
    • Yes, you can purchase Sitecore training on behalf of others without purchasing for yourself. During checkout, you will need to change your email to someone else’s if purchasing by Credit Card. If you plan to purchase on behalf of your team via invoice, please contact globaltraining@sitecore.com.
  • What forms of payment do you take purchasing Sitecore training online?
    • For online purchases, we only take Credit Card.
  • How can I purchase Sitecore training via invoice or PO?
    • For Sitecore training contract purchases, please email globaltraining@sitecore.com.
  • How do I register for a specific instructor-led training session?
    • Once you purchase instructor-led training, you will be enrolled into the course. You will then be able to self-enroll into a session of your choice within your Sitecore Learning account.
  • Where can I purchase Sitecore Certification Exams a la carte?
    • To purchase Sitecore Certification Exams with Credit Card, you can checkout directly on Kryterion Webassessor: https://webassessor.com/sitecore
    • To purchase Sitecore Certification Exams via training contract, please email globaltraining@sitecore.com with the quantity of each exam you are interested in purchasing.
  • When will I be provided with a certification voucher with the purchase of a bundle or Professional Plus Learning Subscription?
    • You will be emailed a certification voucher within 2 weeks of enrollment into the bundle or subscription.
  • Who can I contact for further support?
    • For questions related to your learning or login support, please put in a ticket to the Sitecore Support & Self-Service Portal. To put in a training-related support ticket, please follow the instructions for How to Contact Sitecore Learning Support. 
    • For general questions and questions related to purchasing Sitecore training, please contact globaltraining@sitecore.com.
  • I have enrolled into the Professional/Professional Plus Subscription, can I transfer this enrollment over to another user?
    • Yes, you are welcome to transfer an enrollment as long it is requested within 2 months from the enrollment date, as long as neither the included Instructor Led Training and/or the voucher code has been used. Please reach out the globaltraining@sitecore.com with the name and email address of the user you wish to transfer the training to.
